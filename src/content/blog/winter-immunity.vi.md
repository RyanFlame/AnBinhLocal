As the temperature drops, our bodies become more susceptible to seasonal illnesses. Identifying the right natural ingredients can make a significant difference in how we recover and maintain our health.

## Nature's Cabinet

1. **Gừng (Ginger)**: Known for its warming properties and ability to settle the stomach.
2. **Nghệ (Turmeric)**: A powerful anti-inflammatory that supports overall cellular health.

### Conclusion

Integrating these herbs into your daily tea or meals can provide the protective layer your body needs during the harsh winter months.
